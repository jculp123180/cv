Justin Culp

Missionary and Salesforce Administrator at Encountering the World of Islam.

After graduating with a Bachelor of Science in Advanced Biblical Studies at Calvary University, and then with Master of Arts in Theology and Missions from Midwestern Theological Seminary, I spent 12 years as a missionary in South Sudan. For two years I was living in Dinka cattle camps and doing Bible Storying and pastoral training, and then after getting married, raising support and going back, spent the rest of my time in a remote village called Lohutok where I focused on learning a local language, Discovery Bible Study and evangelism, and Pastoral Training. My role on this team (with Pioneers and Africa Inland Mission also invloved logistical support for other missionaries. Since returning to the USA, I have been learning Salesforce and developing a Salesforce org for the Encountering Islam team.
